# Business Questions

The Emergency Department leadership team would like to better understand patient demand, waiting times and operational performance.

This project aims to answer the following business questions:

1. How many patients attend the Emergency Department each day, week and month?

2. Which hours of the day experience the highest patient demand?

3. Which days of the week are the busiest?

4. What is the average waiting time before a patient is first seen by a clinician?

5. Which triage categories experience the longest waiting times?

6. What percentage of patients are seen within their recommended triage target?

7. How long do patients stay in the Emergency Department?

8. Which arrival methods contribute most to Emergency Department demand?

9. During which shifts are staffing levels lowest compared with patient demand?

10. Which operational recommendations could improve Emergency Department performance?
